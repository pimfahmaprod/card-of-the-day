Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Card of the Day ("we", "our", "the app") is a free tarot card reading web application. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Facebook Login (optional): If you choose to log in with Facebook, we receive your public profile name and profile picture. We do not access your friends list, posts, or any other Facebook data.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data such as card picks, comments, and feature interactions to improve the app experience. This data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database.

Local Storage: We store preferences (language, display name, draw history) in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment or financial information
  • Precise location data
  • Device contacts or messages

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Display your name and profile picture in comments
  • Track card pick statistics (anonymous, aggregated)
  • Save your draw history for your personal reference

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Comments you post are visible to other users of the app.

5. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored using Google Firebase with standard security measures. We do not store Facebook access tokens on our servers.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Log out at any time to disconnect your Facebook account
  • Clear your browser's local storage to remove all locally stored data
  • Use the app without logging in

7. Third-Party Services

  • Facebook Login – governed by Meta's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase – governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages – hosting provider, governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
