Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Card of the Day respects your right to control your personal data. If you want to delete your data associated with our app, please follow the instructions below.

What Data We Store

When you log in with Facebook, we store the following in our database:

  • Your Facebook user ID (used as an identifier)
  • Your display name and profile picture URL
  • Comments and replies you have posted
  • Your card draw history

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1: Remove Local Data

  1. Open Card of the Day
  2. Click the Logout button in your profile dropdown
  3. This will clear all locally stored data (name, picture, draw history) from your browser

Step 2: Remove Facebook Connection

  1. Go to your Facebook App Settings
  2. Find "Card of the Day" in the list of apps
  3. Click Remove to revoke access

Step 3: Request Server Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from our database (comments, replies, draw history, and profile), please contact us through one of these channels:

  • Open an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Include your Facebook display name so we can locate your data

Note: We will process your deletion request within 30 days. Once deleted, your comments, replies, draw history, and profile information will be permanently removed from our database.

Automatic Data

We do not store Facebook access tokens. Anonymous card pick counts are aggregated and cannot be traced back to individual users.
